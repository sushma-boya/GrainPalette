GrainPalette is an AI-powered web app that classifies rice grains into 5 unique types using deep learning and computer vision.

Upload a grain image and get instant, accurate results — powered by transfer learning and a custom-trained CNN model.

This model can detect rice types using deep learning and computer vision.

Accuracy: 97%

Dataset sourced from Kaggle. Built using MobileNetV2 with transfer learning.

✨ Why GrainPalette?

  • 🔍 Classifies 5 popular rice types: Arborio, Basmati, Ipsala, Jasmine, and Karacadag
  • 📸 Instant predictions from uploaded grain images
  • 🧠 Powered by deep learning with transfer learning (MobileNetV2)
  • 🌐 Works directly in your browser – no installs required

🌾 Supported Rice Varieties

  • 🍚 Arborio – Plump, short-grain rice ideal for creamy risottos
  • 🍚 Basmati – Long-grain, aromatic rice loved across Asia
  • 🍚 Ipsala – Turkish medium-grain rice used in pilafs
  • 🍚 Jasmine – Fragrant, soft rice perfect for Thai cuisine
  • 🍚 Karacadag – Rare variety grown in Southeast Turkey

GrainPalette is part of a smart agriculture initiative to improve rice sorting and supply chain automation using AI and computer vision.
